I am a linguist, studying how language is used by different speaker communities. I enjoy observing all kinds of linguistic phenomena in different languages. My research focuses on understanding the processes of word production and comprehension. I am especially interested in how fine-grained phonetic realizations in pronunciation are aligned with subtle lexical and contextual meaning distinction, and whether there exists any cross-linguistic difference. My research methods usually involve conducting behavioral experiments and running computational simulations.
